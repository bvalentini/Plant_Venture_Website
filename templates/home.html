About

Plants 4 Pups is a team of five passionate students from the Entreprenuership Program at NAIT University, bringing together a love for plants and animals. We believe that greenery should be easy, affordable, and accessible to everyone, which is why we offer sustainable, easy-to-grow seedlings at local farmers' markets. Each plant comes with simple care instructions, making it perfect for both beginners and experienced gardeners alike.

But we’re not just about plants—we’re about making a difference. Every single dollar we raise goes directly to Zoe’s Animal Rescue, helping provide food, shelter, and medical care for animals in need. By purchasing a plant, you’re not just adding some greenery to your space—you’re supporting a great cause.Together, we can grow a greener future while giving back to the animals that need us most. So come visit us, pick out a plant, and help make an impact!

Zoe's Animal Rescue

Zoe’s Animal Rescue is a volunteer-run, shelterless organization based in Edmonton, Alberta, dedicated to rescuing and rehoming animals in need. Focused primarily on dogs and cats, the rescue operates without a physical shelter, relying on foster homes to care for animals until they find their forever families. Volunteers play a crucial role in the organization, providing not only care for the animals but also offering their skills and time for various tasks such as transportation, social media, and fundraising. Zoe’s Animal Rescue aims to prevent animal overpopulation and reduce the number of animals in need by promoting adoption and fostering. In addition to providing loving homes, the organization advocates for responsible pet ownership, spaying, and neutering. With a strong commitment to animal welfare, Zoe’s Animal Rescue is making a significant impact in the local community, giving abandoned and stray animals a chance at a better life.
